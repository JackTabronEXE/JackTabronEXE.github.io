DISCUSS HOW UNDERSTANDING THE PRINCIPLES OF NEUROPLASTICITY BENEFITS PEOPLE:

Neuroplasticity refers to our brains ability to learn new skills or rewire itself following experiences or injury, for example those who may experience losing function of a certain part of their brain following a car accident can then redevelop that function of their brain in another part of their brain. The former refers to things like learning new languages (which children can do easily and adults cannot, for example a programming language LOL).

EQ & IQ

APRIL 30, 2022


WHAT IS EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE?

Emotional Intelligence or EQ is a combination of being a) generally socially aware, b) being able to read and understand others emotions and applying what you have learned to how you would approach an interaction with that person and lastly being self-aware enough to apply the above logic to ones own emotional response. Often this is the most difficult point to accomplish as many individuals have difficulty in rationalizing emotions with logic and/or fact paticularly when they are experiencing pressure.


HOW IS EQ DIFFERENT FROM IQ?


EQ is a way that one can measure how in-tune they are with their own emotions and rationalizing them with logic as well as recoginizing others emotions, utilizing the effective management of these emotions can lead to being able to work more productively as part of a team where as IQ is a finite value in which you can measure someones ability to understand, learn and apply information and skills. The key comparison of EQ vs IQ is how well they balance logical decision making over emotional decision making.


WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?


It is important to anaylse EQ and IQ when assessing an individuals strength or weaknessess. For example someone with IQ theoretically would work more productively in a situation without much interaction or co-oeprative based projects. Where as someone with high eq would theoretically be better working with and managing others so they would be better suited for a management position.

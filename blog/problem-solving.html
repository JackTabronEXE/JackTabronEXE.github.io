I was was stuck on a technical coding problem using techniques/methods that I have learned/practised previously, my first choice of problem solving techniques was to utilize 'pseudocode' whereby you write simple comments (like on a marked-up document) throughout the code. You use this to break the problem down into smaller tasks that you can work on one by one and hopefully reach your end goal. I felt confident throughout the process that I would reach what I set out to acheive if I really put my mind to it. I learned that whenever your perplexed about a problem it's good to focus on what your trying to acheive and all of the individual steps and path you need to take in order to get there.
